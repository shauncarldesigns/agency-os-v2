"Just a mockup. Not live - just something I put together for you so you could see how the layout works, how your services might be laid out, how the messaging might sound. I just want you to see it. See if it feels like your company. See if it actually shows who you are and what you do in a way that you like."

> Use when they react to "you built me a website?" Keep the language careful: mockup, not live, no assumption that they approved anything.
