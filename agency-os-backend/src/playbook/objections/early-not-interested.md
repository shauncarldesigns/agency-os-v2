"Totally fair — and I get it, you don't know me yet. All I was going to ask is one question about how business is coming in right now. If it's not useful, I'll get out of your hair. Fair?"

> Pre-reveal. This is the "before you've earned any credibility" not-interested — different from the demo-ask stage's final rejection. Keep it low-pressure. If they say no again, thank them and end the call. Do NOT mention websites.
