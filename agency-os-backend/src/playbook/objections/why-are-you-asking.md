"Fair question. I work with local businesses around customer acquisition, but I don't want to assume you have a problem that isn't there. I was just trying to understand how business is coming in now."

Then pivot:

"Would you say most of it comes from referrals, Google, social media, or something else?"

> Pre-reveal objection. Do NOT say "I build websites" or "I already built you a website" or "I noticed you don't have a website." The whole point of Question-Oriented mode is that the solution stays hidden until you've identified a real problem.
